Peanut Butter High Calorie Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • 320ml milk / 330g milk
    • 1 ml milk isn't 1g milk, So that's why 320ml milk shouldn't be mistaken to be 320g milk.
  • 32g Oats
  • 32g Peanut Butter
  • 4-5g Coco Powder
  • 4-5g Honey

How to make?

Just add it all in a jar and blend for 45-60 secs or accordingly till smooth liquidy texture.
